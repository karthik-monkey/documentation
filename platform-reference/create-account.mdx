### Step 1: Visit the Website

Visit our website here, and click the **sign in** button on the top right corner.

### Step 2: Choose Sign Up Option

You can directly sign in with your Google account, or you can create an account. To create an account, click on **"Sign Up"** on the bottom left of the login screen.
