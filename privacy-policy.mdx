This Privacy Policy explains how Synapse AI, Inc. and its affiliates ("Synapse AI," "we," "us," or "our") collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our website, platform, mobile applications, APIs, SDKs, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). We distinguish between our direct customers ("you") and your end-users to ensure clarity in our data practices. By accessing or using the Services, you agree to the terms of this Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use immediately.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Customer Information
When you sign up for or use the Services, we may collect:

- **Contact Details**: Name, email address, phone number, company/organization name, job title, billing address
- **Account Credentials**: Username, password, and authentication tokens obtained via Google, GitHub, or other supported authentication providers
- **Demographics & Preferences**: Optional information you choose to provide, such as your role, industry, use case preferences, or profile picture
- **Billing Information**: Payment method details, billing history, and transaction records (processed securely through our payment processors)

### 1.2 Training Data and Model Information
As part of your use of our fine-tuning platform, we may process:

- **Training Datasets**: Text, image, tabular, or other data types you upload for model fine-tuning
- **Model Configurations**: Hyperparameters, adapter settings, training preferences, and deployment configurations
- **Model Weights and Artifacts**: Custom model weights, adapters, and related files you create through our platform
- **Performance Metrics**: Training progress, model accuracy, inference statistics, and optimization results

**Important**: You retain full ownership of your training data and custom model weights. We process this information solely to provide our Services and do not use your proprietary data to train our own models or share it with third parties.

### 1.3 Usage & Technical Data
We automatically collect certain technical information to operate and improve our Services:

- **Device & Browser Info**: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device identifiers
- **Platform Usage**: Pages viewed, features used, training jobs initiated, model deployments, API calls made
- **Performance Data**: Response times, error rates, system performance metrics, resource utilization
- **Log Data**: Server logs, security events, session information, and diagnostic data
- **Cookies & Similar Technologies**: Cookies, local storage, session tokens, and authentication credentials

### 1.4 Third-Party Sources
We may receive information about you from third parties who support our platform operations, including:

- Authentication providers (Google, GitHub)
- Payment processors and billing services
- Analytics and monitoring services
- Cloud infrastructure providers
- Marketing and communication platforms

### 5.1 Data Access & Control
- **Access & Correction**: You may request a copy of your personal information or request corrections to inaccurate data
- **Data Portability**: You can export your training data, model weights, and configurations at any time
- **Deletion**: You can request deletion of your personal data; note that this will terminate your access to the Services and may affect your ability to access previously trained models

### 5.2 Communication Preferences

- **Model Ownership**: You retain full ownership and control over your custom models and training data

## 6. Data Security & Your Risks

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data:

- **Encryption**: Data in transit and at rest is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access controls and multi-factor authentication
- **Infrastructure Security**: Secure cloud infrastructure with regular security audits
- **Model Isolation**: Training jobs and model data are isolated between customers
- **Compliance**: SOC 2 Type II and other relevant security certifications

However, no system is completely secure. By using our Services, you acknowledge and accept residual security risks inherent in internet-based services.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the Services:

- **Personal Information**: Retained for the duration of your account plus up to 7 years for legal compliance
- **Training Data**: Retained according to your specified preferences or until account deletion
- **Model Weights**: Retained until you delete them or terminate your account
- **Usage Analytics**: Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for platform improvement
- **Legal Compliance**: Some data may be retained longer to comply with legal obligations

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 16, please contact us immediately and we will promptly delete it.

## 10. California Residents' Privacy Rights

Under the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), California residents have the right to:

- Know what personal information we collect and how it's used
- Request deletion of personal information
- Opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
- Request correction of inaccurate personal information
- Opt-out of automated decision-making

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@synapseai.com. For unresolved disputes, you may contact the California Privacy Protection Agency.
